The Golden Mug

The Golden Mug, also known as TGM, is a structured café-style Roblox experience focused on professionalism, roleplay, and community engagement. Players can work, train, serve customers, and progress through a clear staff ranking system while management and ownership guide operations through organized tools and internal systems. A polished café experience built around structure

TGM is designed to feel organized, active, and rewarding. Whether someone is visiting as a customer or joining the team as an employee, the experience is meant to reflect professionalism, consistency, and an engaging community atmosphere.

Roleplay and service

Players can serve customers, participate in training, and help create an immersive café environment that feels active and welcoming.

Clear progression

Staff progress through a defined rank structure with room for development, responsibility, and increased trust over time.

Organized leadership

Management and ownership use structured tools and systems to oversee operations, maintain standards, and support the community.

What the employee portal supports

The TGM Portal is built around staff operations and internal workflow. It supports leave of absence management, staff management, signup and login approvals, and task management with role-based access for Employees, Admins, Owners, and the Portal Developer.

Leave of Absence

Employees can request leave and authorized leadership can review, approve, or deny requests inside the portal.

Staff Management

Admins and higher roles can review staff information, monitor access, and help manage the team based on rank permissions.

Tasks and requests

Leadership can assign tasks and review pending signup requests from employees whose Discord IDs are already present in the datastore.

Staff access starts here

Staff members can sign in with their Roblox Username and Password. New accounts require approval unless they are pre-approved. Your portal uses Firestore for user records, signup requests, tasks, leave records, and password storage.
